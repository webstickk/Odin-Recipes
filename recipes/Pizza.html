Pizza Recipe

Home

Ingredients:

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • envelope Fleischmann's® Pizza Crust Yeast
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • ½ cup pizza sauce, or to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste>
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste
  • 1 ¼ cups water

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Start a charcoal fire or preheat a gas grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2: Combine 2 cups flour, undissolved yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and 1/3 cup oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute. Gradually add enough flour to make a soft dough that forms a ball and is slightly sticky. Knead on a floured surface, adding additional flour if necessary, until smooth and elastic but not sticky, about 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Divide dough into 8 portions. Pat or roll dough on a well-floured counter to about 8-inch circles; they do not need to be perfect.
  4. Step 4:Brush both sides of crusts with additional oil. Using hands, lift each crust carefully and place on grill. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes until bottoms are lightly browned and tops look set. Using long handled tongs, remove crusts from grill and place on a baking sheet with grilled-sides facing up.
  5. Step 5: Lightly add sauce to the top of each grilled crust, then top with mozzarella cheese.
  6. step 6: Carefully slide pizzas back onto the grill. Cook an additional 3 to 4 minutes until bottom crusts are browned and cheese is melted. Remove from the grill and serve immediately.
